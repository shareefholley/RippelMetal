Rippel Metal is a retail store specializing in architectural detail, design, fabrication and contemporary home furnishings. You can purchase items in our showroom, order from catalogs, or have us craft custom furniture by local artists.
Rippel Metal is a retail store that caters to people with a more contemporary aesthetic. We carry furniture, lighting, tableware, art and other home furnishings. Custom Design

Effective custom design hinges on the relationship developed between the Rippel Metal team and their customers. The custom furniture piece is dependent on the relationship between form and function, creating something beautiful yet practical. The final product should also take into account the relationship between the piece and its environment. Architectural design and custom furniture is what Rippel Metal does best.
